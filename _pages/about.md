Hi there! I'm Haoyang (William) Wu, a junior student majoring in Computer Science at the University of Michigan. I'm also pursuing a dual degree in Mechanical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), with an expected graduation in 2027.

My research interests lie primarily in multimodal learning. I am currently a research intern at Princeton University, advised by [Prof. Zhuang Liu](https://liuzhuang13.github.io). I also work as a research intern in the [ARM Lab](https://arm.robotics.umich.edu) directed by [Prof. Dmitry Berenson](https://berenson.robotics.umich.edu). At SJTU, I was deeply engaged in surgical robotics research at the [SIRIUS Lab](https://banyutong.github.io/sirius_lab_website/), mentored by [Prof. Yutong Ban](https://people.csail.mit.edu/yban/). I have also had the pleasure of working with many exceptional researchers, including [Dr. Gabe Sarch](https://gabesarch.me), [Abhinav Kumar](https://abhinav-kumar-99.github.io), [Mark Vander Merwe](https://mvandermerwe.github.io), and [Dr. Johnson Tsun-hsuan Wang](https://zswang666.github.io). You can find my resume (updated 10/17/2025) [here](https://william-hywu.github.io/files/Resume_Haoyang Wu_internship.pdf)

**I will be joining Princeton as visiting intern this summer! See you in Princeton**
